Greenskeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Greenskeeper (“Greenskeeper,” “the App,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the Greenskeeper iOS application and any related premium subscription features branded as Greenskeeper+.

1. Summary

Greenskeeper is designed to work without creating a developer-hosted account. We do not intentionally collect, sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising. User content such as saved golf courses, badges, notes, and photos is stored on the user’s device and, if enabled by the user, in the user’s personal iCloud account through Apple’s services.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, or payment card information through our own servers.

Greenskeeper may process or access the following information locally on your device in order to provide app functionality:

  • Golf courses, badges, notes, and other content you create or save in the app
  • Photos you choose to add to your entries
  • Subscription status information returned by Apple so the app can determine whether Greenskeeper+ features should be unlocked
  • iCloud file availability information needed to support optional backup and restore features

This information is used only to operate the app’s features and is not uploaded to developer-controlled servers.

3. Local Device Storage

Greenskeeper stores user-generated content locally on your Apple device so the app can function. Local device storage is under your control and may remain on your device until you delete the app or remove that content.

4. iCloud Backup and Restore

If you enable backup or restore features, Greenskeeper may save files to your personal iCloud account using Apple’s iCloud services. This storage is provided by Apple and is controlled by your Apple ID and Apple account settings.

We do not receive or maintain a copy of those backup files on our own servers. Availability of iCloud features depends on your device settings, Apple account status, and Apple’s services.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

Greenskeeper offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions through Apple’s In-App Purchase system under the Greenskeeper+ name. All billing, payment processing, renewal handling, and cancellation controls are managed by Apple. We do not collect or store your full payment card details or Apple account credentials.

We may receive limited transaction-related information from Apple, such as product identifiers, purchase status, renewal status, or expiration information, solely to determine whether premium features should be available in the app.

6. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Greenskeeper does not intentionally use third-party advertising SDKs, sell personal information, or use personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those practices are implemented.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or disclose personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Information may be handled by Apple when you use Apple-provided services such as iCloud and In-App Purchase. Those services are governed by Apple’s own terms and privacy disclosures.

8. Data Retention

Because Greenskeeper does not maintain user content on developer-controlled servers, retention is generally determined by your device, your iCloud account, and your own actions. Data stored locally may remain until deleted from the app or until the app is removed. Data stored in iCloud may remain until removed from your iCloud account or overwritten by later backups.

9. Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to use premium subscription features.
  • You can cancel a Greenskeeper+ subscription at any time through your Apple account settings.
  • You can disable iCloud for the app in your Apple settings, subject to Apple’s platform controls.
  • You can delete local app data by deleting content in the app or removing the app from your device.

10. Children’s Privacy

Greenskeeper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through developer-controlled systems.

11. Colorado Privacy Notice

The Colorado Privacy Act (“CPA”) applies to certain entities that conduct business in Colorado or target Colorado residents and that either process the personal data of more than 100,000 Colorado consumers in a calendar year or derive revenue from the sale of personal data and process or control the personal data of at least 25,000 consumers. Greenskeeper is not designed to sell personal data, and based on the app’s current design we do not believe Greenskeeper is operating as a data-selling business. However, we provide this notice for transparency and to support future compliance if our practices change.

If the CPA applies to Greenskeeper now or in the future, Colorado residents may have rights under applicable law, which can include rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a portable copy of personal data, and rights to opt out of certain processing such as the sale of personal data or targeted advertising. Because Greenskeeper does not maintain developer-hosted user profiles or sell personal data, many of these rights may not be relevant to the app’s current operation.

Colorado residents may contact us using the information below with any privacy request or question.

12. California Notice

Greenskeeper does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are commonly used in U.S. privacy laws such as the CCPA and CPRA.

13. International Users

Greenskeeper is offered through Apple’s App Store and may be downloaded in multiple regions. If you use the app outside the United States, you understand that Apple services you choose to use, including iCloud and In-App Purchase, may process information according to Apple’s systems and policies.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version at this page and update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of Greenskeeper after an updated policy becomes effective means you acknowledge the updated policy.
